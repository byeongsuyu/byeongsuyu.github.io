Category Theory Seminar, Summer 2019

This page collects all information about Category Theory Seminar, Summer 2019 at Texas A&M University, organized by me. The main text is Dr. Riehl's ``Category Theory in context." This will be held regularly on Monday and Thursday 12:00pm-12:45pm in June and July at Blocker 624.

Schedule

  • Jun 3, 2019, Lecture 1 (Byeongsu Yu) Definition of Category
  • Jun 6, 2019, Lecture 2 (Byeongsu Yu) Duality
  • Jun 10, 2019, Lecture 3 (Byeongsu Yu) Duality and Functoriality
  • Jun 13, 2019, Lecture 4 (Byeongsu Yu) Functoriality and Exercises
  • Jun 17, 2019, Lecture 5 (John Weeks) Naturality
  • Jun 20, 2019, Lecture 6 (Byeongsu Yu) Exercises in Functoriality and Naturality
  • Jun 24, 2019, Lecture 7 (Alexander Weigandt) Equivalence
  • Jun 27, 2019, Lecture 8 (Byeongsu Yu) Exercises in Equivalence
  • Jul 1, 2019, Lecture 9 (Tolulope Oke) Diagram chasing
  • Jul 9, 2019, Lecture 10 (Byeongsu Yu) 2-Categories and Exercises
  • Jul 11, 2019, Lecture 11 (Byeongsu Yu) Representable Functor
  • Jul 16, 2019, Lecture 12 (Byeongsu Yu) Yoneda Lemma
  • Jul 18, 2019, Lecture 13 (Byeongsu Yu) Universal Properties and elements
  • Jul 23, 2019, Lecture 14 (Byeongsu Yu) Limits and Colimits
  • Jul 25, 2019, Lecture 15 (Byeongsu Yu) Limits and Colimits in set category
  • Jul 30, 2019, Lecture 16 (Zheng Kuang) Adjoint Functor
